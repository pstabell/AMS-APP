# Agency Platform - Getting Started Video Script

## Video Overview
**Duration:** 3-5 minutes
**Target Audience:** Insurance agencies evaluating the platform
**Goal:** Demonstrate the core value proposition and ease of use

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00 - 0:15)
**[Screen: Agency Platform login page]**

"Welcome to the Agency Platform - your all-in-one solution for managing commissions, tracking performance, and integrating with your existing insurance tech stack."

### Demo Mode Introduction (0:15 - 0:45)
**[Screen: Login with demo credentials]**

"Let me show you around using our demo environment. I'll log in with a demo password to show you what the platform looks like with real data."

**[Action: Enter password and log in]**

"Once logged in, you're immediately taken to your Agency Dashboard - your command center for all agency operations."

### Agency Dashboard Tour (0:45 - 1:30)
**[Screen: Agency Dashboard with metrics]**

"The dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your most important metrics:

- **Total Premium Volume** across all your agencies
- **Commission Earned** - track your revenue in real-time
- **Active Policies** - see how many policies you're managing
- **Agency Count** - if you're managing multiple locations

Below that, we have interactive charts showing:
- Premium trends over time
- Commission breakdown by agency
- Policy distribution across your book of business
- Monthly performance trends"

**[Action: Hover over charts to show interactivity]**

"All of these charts are interactive - you can hover to see exact numbers, zoom in on specific time periods, and filter by agency."

### Agency Performance (1:30 - 2:00)
**[Screen: Scroll to agency performance table]**

"Further down, you'll see a detailed breakdown of each agency's performance:
- Premium volume per agency
- Commission rates
- Policy counts
- Performance trends

This makes it easy to identify your top performers and agencies that might need additional support."

### Integrations Catalog (2:00 - 2:45)
**[Screen: Navigate to Integrations page]**

"One of the most powerful features is our integration ecosystem. Click on 'Integrations' in the sidebar."

**[Screen: Integrations catalog]**

"Here you can browse our catalog of 20+ integrations with leading insurance technology platforms:
- **Carrier Systems** like Applied Epic, Vertafore, and Hawksoft
- **CRM Platforms** like Salesforce and HubSpot
- **Accounting Software** like QuickBooks and Xero
- **Communication Tools** like Slack and Microsoft Teams

Each integration is categorized and searchable, making it easy to find the tools you already use."

**[Action: Click on an integration to show details]**

"Click on any integration to see detailed information about what it does, setup instructions, and documentation."

### Agency Onboarding (2:45 - 3:15)
**[Screen: Navigate to Agency Onboarding]**

"When you're ready to add a new agency to the platform, our streamlined onboarding process makes it simple."

**[Screen: Show onboarding form]**

"Just fill out the basic agency information:
- Agency name and contact details
- Commission structure
- Integration preferences
- Subscription tier

And you're done! The agency is immediately ready to start tracking commissions and using the platform."

### Multi-Tenancy & Security (3:15 - 3:45)
**[Screen: Return to dashboard]**

"One thing to note - the platform is built with enterprise-grade security and multi-tenancy in mind. Each agency's data is completely isolated using Row Level Security.

This means:
- Agencies only see their own data
- Admins can see aggregate metrics across all agencies
- All data is encrypted and protected
- We're compliant with insurance industry security standards"

### Closing (3:45 - 4:00)
**[Screen: Dashboard overview]**

"That's a quick tour of the Agency Platform. With real-time commission tracking, powerful integrations, and an intuitive interface, we make it easy to manage your entire agency operation in one place.

Ready to see how it works with your data? Contact us to schedule a personalized demo or start your free trial today."

**[Screen: Contact information or call-to-action]**

## Technical Setup for Recording

1. **Browser Setup:**
   - Use Chrome in Incognito mode (clean, no extensions)
   - Set browser zoom to 100%
   - Hide bookmarks bar
   - Full screen the Streamlit app

2. **Demo Data:**
   - Ensure DEMO_MODE=true in .env
   - Run on localhost:8503 (agency-platform branch)
   - Pre-load the dashboard before recording

3. **Screen Recording:**
   - Record at 1920x1080 (1080p)
   - 60fps for smooth animations
   - Record system audio if adding voiceover live
   - Use OBS Studio or similar professional screen recording software

4. **Cursor:**
   - Use a cursor highlighting tool to make mouse movements visible
   - Move cursor slowly and deliberately
   - Pause on important elements for 1-2 seconds
